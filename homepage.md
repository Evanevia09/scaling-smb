# Homepage Blueprint: Scaling SMB – The Growth Engine

## 1. Goal

Establish **Scaling SMB** as the premier partner for local business growth. Convert visitors into **Free Digital Growth Audit** leads by demonstrating speed (7 days) and predictability.

## 2. Branding & Visuals

* **Brand Name:** Scaling SMB
* **Tagline:** The Predictable Customer Acquisition Machine.
* **Colors:** Modern Dark Mode. Deep Charcoal (`#121212`), Pure White text, and **"Scaling Blue"** (`#3B82F6`) or **"Growth Green"** (`#10B981`) for CTAs.
* **Typography:** Clean Sans-serif (Inter or Montserrat) for a tech-forward, reliable feel.

* **Headline:** `Scaling SMBs with a Predictable Acquisition Machine.`

* **Secondary CTA:** "See the Scaling System" (Scrolls to Process section).

* **Speed:** From zero to launched in 7 days.
