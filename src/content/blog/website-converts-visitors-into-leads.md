Traffic without conversions is expensive noise. For local businesses, the goal is simple: get the right visitor to call, book, or request a quote in under 60 seconds.

## 1. Put your phone number above the fold

On mobile, your tap-to-call button should be visible without scrolling. Many sites hide contact options in the footer—where almost nobody looks first.

## 2. One clear primary action per page

Every page should answer: *What should I do next?* Pick one action—book a call, get a quote, or schedule service—and repeat it in the hero, mid-page, and footer.

## 3. Show proof early

Add reviews, before/after photos, or client logos near the top. Local buyers trust other local buyers more than marketing copy.

## 4. Speed is trust

If your site loads slowly on mobile, visitors assume your service is slow too. Compress images, lazy-load galleries, and test on a real phone—not just desktop.

## 5. Match the message to the ad or search

If someone clicks an ad for "emergency plumber Macau," the landing page should say exactly that—not a generic homepage.

## Quick audit checklist

- Can I call or book in one tap on mobile?
- Is the offer obvious in 5 seconds?
- Do I see social proof before I scroll far?
- Does the page load in under 3 seconds?

Fix these five items before spending more on ads. Conversion rate improvements often beat traffic increases.
